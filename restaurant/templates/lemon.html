🍋 Our Story

A little kitchen.
A big obsession.

Little Lemon started when our founder Ama couldn't stop making lemon drizzle cakes for everyone she knew. Friends turned into regulars, regulars turned into a waitlist, and the waitlist turned into this. We use only fresh lemons, local ingredients, and old family recipes. No shortcuts. No fake flavours.
